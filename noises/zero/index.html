zero

 

Zero was born in South London in 1958.

He learned how to do the twist in 1962. Thanks mum…

He bought his first piece of vinyl in 1970 (from W H Smith’s in Forest Hill).

He joined his first band (as a ‘non-musician’) in 1974.

He changed his name in 1985.

He started DJing around about 1991.

He joined his second band (as the singer) in 1993.

He moved to Manchester in 2001.

He bought a pair of second hand Technics SL-1200 Mk2’s from Johnny Roadhouse in 2006.

He started seriously collecting 7” vinyl in 2008. Thanks sis…

He moved back to London in 2016.

He made his first appearance on ExileFm in 2017.

He is a cyclist and and a supporter of Crystal Palace FC.
